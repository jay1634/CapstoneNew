AIRPORT TRANSFER RULES
======================

PICKUP WINDOW
-------------
- Domestic Arrivals:
  - Driver reporting time: 30 minutes before scheduled arrival (where feasible).
  - Free waiting time after flight landing: Up to 60 minutes.

- International Arrivals:
  - Free waiting time after flight landing: Up to 90 minutes.

EXTRA WAITING CHARGES
---------------------
- Beyond free waiting period:
  - Additional waiting charges may apply, depending on city and vehicle type.
  - Typically charged in 30-minute slabs.

NO-SHOW CONDITIONS
------------------
- If passenger is unreachable on:
  - Provided mobile number
  - WhatsApp
  for more than:
  - 60 minutes (domestic) / 90 minutes (international),
  AND has not informed of delay,
  then:
  - It may be treated as a "No-Show" and transfer may be forfeited.

LUGGAGE POLICY
--------------
- Standard sedans:
  - Suitable for 2–3 adults + 2 medium suitcases.
- For additional luggage:
  - Customers should inform at time of booking to arrange appropriate vehicle.

NIGHT CHARGES
-------------
- Some airports/cities may levy:
  - Night surcharge for pickups between 23:00–05:00.
- This will be informed at the time of booking.

EMERGENCY CONTACT
-----------------
- In case of:
  - Flight delays
  - Immigration delays
  - Baggage delays
- Customers are requested to:
  - Inform SkyTrail support or the driver contact shared in the voucher as soon as possible.

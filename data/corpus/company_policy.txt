COMPANY POLICIES
================

1. BOOKING POLICY
-----------------
- A booking is considered "confirmed" only after:
  - Written confirmation from SkyTrail Journeys via email or WhatsApp, AND
  - Advance payment has been received and acknowledged.

- Advance Payment:
  - Domestic trips: Minimum 30% of total package cost.
  - International trips: Minimum 40–50% of total package cost (varies by destination and airline rules).

- Balance Payment:
  - Domestic: Due 7 days before departure.
  - International: Due 14 days before departure.
  - Last-minute bookings (within 7 days of travel) may require 100% payment upfront.

- Payment Modes:
  - UPI, net banking, credit/debit cards, and approved payment gateways.
  - We do NOT encourage cash above regulatory limits.

2. PRICING & INCLUSIONS POLICY
------------------------------
- Package prices are based on:
  - Season and travel dates
  - Hotel category and room type
  - Number of travellers and occupancy
  - Inclusions (flights, meals, sightseeing, transfers, etc.)

- What is clearly mentioned as “Included” in the itinerary/quotation is covered.
- Anything not specifically listed under “Inclusions” is treated as an “Exclusion”.
- Government taxes, tolls, parking and service charges may be included or excluded as per final quote.

3. DOCUMENTATION POLICY
-----------------------
- For all travellers:
  - Government-approved ID is mandatory for hotel check-in (Aadhaar, Passport, DL, etc.).
- For air travel:
  - Name on ticket must match Government ID.
  - For international trips, passport validity of at least 6 months from date of travel is recommended.
- Customers are responsible for:
  - Ensuring passport, visa, and other documents are valid and correct.

4. CHILD & SENIOR TRAVELLER POLICY
----------------------------------
- Children:
  - Child policy varies by airline and hotel.
  - Parents/guardians must supervise children at all times.
- Seniors:
  - We recommend disclosing any major medical conditions.
  - Certain activities (treks, water sports, etc.) may not be advisable.

5. HEALTH & SAFETY POLICY
-------------------------
- We recommend:
  - Basic travel insurance for all travellers.
  - Avoiding self-driving in unknown hill/forest regions at night.
  - Following local laws, beach safety flags and official advisories.

- During pandemics/outbreaks:
  - Additional rules (RT-PCR, vaccination proof, masks) may apply as per government guidelines.
  - Such requirements are the traveller’s responsibility to fulfil.

6. BEHAVIOUR & CONDUCT POLICY
-----------------------------
- We expect all travellers to:
  - Respect local culture, customs and dress codes where applicable.
  - Avoid aggressive, abusive or threatening behaviour towards staff, drivers or guides.
  - Follow hotel and transport rules (e.g., no smoking in rooms or vehicles where prohibited).

- SkyTrail Journeys and partners reserve the right to:
  - Refuse service to travellers displaying violent, abusive or unsafe behaviour.

7. DATA & PRIVACY POLICY (SUMMARY)
----------------------------------
- We collect:
  - Basic contact details (name, email, phone)
  - Travel preferences and ID details (for bookings)
- Purpose:
  - To process bookings, share itineraries and provide support.
- We do not:
  - Sell customer data to third-party marketers.
- We may:
  - Use anonymised data to analyze trends and improve services.

8. EMERGENCY SUPPORT POLICY
---------------------------
- On-trip emergency support is available 24x7 via:
  - Primary helpline
  - WhatsApp emergency line
- Non-emergency requests (changes, customisations, feedback) are handled during business hours.

9. DISCLOSURE OF LIMITATIONS
----------------------------
- We act as an intermediary between customer and:
  - Airlines, hotels, transport operators and activity providers.
- Delays, cancellations or changes caused by:
  - Weather, strikes, technical failures, government orders, or third-party issues
  are beyond our direct control. We will, however, assist to the best extent possible.

10. POLICY UPDATES
------------------
- Policies may be updated from time to time.
- The version shared at the time of booking will be applicable to that booking.

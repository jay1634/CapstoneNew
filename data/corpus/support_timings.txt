SUPPORT TIMINGS
===============

TIME ZONE
---------
All times mentioned are in:
- IST (Indian Standard Time) UTC +5:30

PRE-BOOKING ENQUIRY SUPPORT
---------------------------
- Phone & WhatsApp:
  - Monday to Saturday: 10:00 AM – 7:00 PM
- Email:
  - Responses typically within 24 hours (working days)

POST-BOOKING & ON-TRIP SUPPORT
------------------------------
- Phone (On-trip Helpline):
  - 24x7 for active travellers with ongoing trips.
- WhatsApp:
  - Priority for travellers currently on trip days.
- Email:
  - Responses within 12–24 hours for on-trip issues, wherever email is used.

NON-EMERGENCY REQUESTS
----------------------
- Changes in plan, future enquiries, feedback:
  - Handled during regular working hours:
    - Monday to Saturday: 10:00 AM – 7:00 PM

SUNDAYS & PUBLIC HOLIDAYS
-------------------------
- Limited staff available.
- Emergency on-trip queries still handled via:
  - Helpline
  - WhatsApp emergency contact
- Non-urgent matters may be addressed on the next working day.

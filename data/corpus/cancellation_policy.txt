CANCELLATION POLICY
===================

GENERAL PRINCIPLES
------------------
- “Cancellation” means the customer voluntarily requests to cancel the trip or a part of it.
- All cancellation requests must be:
  - Sent in writing via email or registered WhatsApp number, AND
  - Acknowledged by SkyTrail Journeys.

- The effective cancellation date will be:
  - The date on which we receive the written cancellation request.

DOMESTIC PACKAGES (WITH HOTEL + TRANSPORT)
------------------------------------------
1. More than 21 days before departure:
   - 10% of total package cost OR actual charges levied by hotels/transport (whichever is higher).

2. 15–21 days before departure:
   - 25% of total package cost.

3. 8–14 days before departure:
   - 40% of total package cost.

4. 4–7 days before departure:
   - 60% of total package cost.

5. Less than 4 days before departure / No Show:
   - 100% of total package cost (no refund).

INTERNATIONAL PACKAGES
----------------------
- Due to strict airline and hotel policies, international cancellations are more restrictive.

1. More than 30 days before departure:
   - 20% of total package cost OR actual third-party charges, whichever is higher.

2. 15–30 days before departure:
   - 40% of total package cost.

3. 7–14 days before departure:
   - 60% of total package cost.

4. Less than 7 days / No Show:
   - 100% of total package cost.

FLIGHT TICKETS (STANDALONE OR WITH PACKAGE)
-------------------------------------------
- Cancellation of air tickets is subject to:
  - Airline fare rules
  - Class of booking (refundable / non-refundable)
- SkyTrail Journeys service fee:
  - Up to ₹500 per person per sector may be charged additionally for processing cancellations.

HOTEL-ONLY BOOKINGS
-------------------
- Strictly as per hotel-specific policy shared at time of booking.
- Non-refundable rates:
  - 100% retention applies in most cases.
- Flexible rates:
  - Typically 1–3 nights retention depending on season.

ACTIVITIES, SIGHTSEEING & ADD-ONS
---------------------------------
- Certain activities (water sports, theme parks, shows, safaris) may be:
  - 100% non-refundable once booked.
- Others may allow partial refund subject to partner rules.

NO-SHOW POLICY
--------------
- If a traveller fails to:
  - Board a flight, OR
  - Check-in at hotel on the date of arrival, OR
  - Report for a pre-booked transfer/activity
  without timely intimation,
  then:
  - It will be considered a No-Show.
  - No refunds will normally be possible.

CANCELLATION DUE TO FORCE MAJEURE
---------------------------------
- In events like:
  - Natural disasters, epidemics, strikes, riots, war, sudden government restrictions
- SkyTrail Journeys will:
  - Assist in rescheduling/bookings where possible.
  - Process refunds strictly as per airline, hotel and vendor policies.
- Additional charges or partial losses may still be incurred.

IMPORTANT NOTES
---------------
- Taxes, convenience fees and payment gateway charges:
  - May not be refundable depending on third-party rules.
- Refund timeline and mode:
  - As per “Refund Policy” document.

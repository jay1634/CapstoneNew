REFUND POLICY
=============

WHEN REFUNDS ARE APPLICABLE
---------------------------
Refunds may be applicable in the following scenarios:
1. Customer-initiated cancellation (as per applicable Cancellation Policy slabs).
2. Reduction in services or number of travellers with prior notice.
3. Over-charging or duplicate payment errors.
4. Partial refunds from airline/hotel due to schedule changes or operational issues.

REFUND SOURCES
--------------
Refunds are processed based on:
- Amount received from third parties (airlines, hotels, vendors) after deductions.
- SkyTrail Journeys’ own service fee deductions wherever applicable and communicated.

REFUND AMOUNT CALCULATION
-------------------------
Refund Amount = Total Amount Paid  
                – Cancellation Charges (as per policy)  
                – Non-refundable components (if any)  
                – Payment gateway charges (if applicable)  

Refunds will NOT include:
- Visa fees already paid to consulates/agents.
- Insurance premiums.
- Non-refundable activity tickets (theme parks, events, etc.) where mentioned.

REFUND TIMELINE
---------------
- Domestic packages:
  - Typically 7–14 working days from confirmation of cancellation and receipt of all vendor credits.
- International packages:
  - 14–21 working days (may be longer if airlines/hotels delay processing).
- Flight tickets:
  - As per airline timeline; we initiate within 3–5 working days of receiving airline credit.

The above timelines are indicative and depend on:
- Banks
- Payment gateways
- Processing time of third party vendors

REFUND MODE
-----------
Refunds are generally processed via:
- Original mode of payment (UPI, card, bank transfer), or
- Bank transfer to the customer’s account, if original mode is unavailable.

WE DO NOT:
----------
- Issue cash refunds for online or digital payments.
- Refund to third-party accounts (must be in the name of primary or authorised traveller).

NON-REFUNDABLE CASES
--------------------
No refund is typically possible when:
- No-show for flights/hotels/activities.
- Non-refundable hotel or special fare ticket rules apply.
- Visa is rejected or delayed due to customer-related reasons.
- Customer fails to provide required documents on time (for visa or bookings).
- Customer does not use part of the services voluntarily (e.g., skips a day tour).

DISPUTE HANDLING
----------------
- In case of any discrepancy between:
  - Vendor credit note and expected refund
- SkyTrail Journeys will:
  - Share vendor communication or screenshots where feasible.
  - Attempt to resolve with vendor on customer’s behalf.

- Final refunds are subject to:
  - Actual credit received from third-party partners.

NOTE
----
This Refund Policy must be read together with:
- Cancellation Policy
- Terms & Conditions
- Package-specific terms shared at the time of booking.
